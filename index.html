Work

Most of my current work is all Excel based. Here are a few of the projects I've created for amazon that have been used across multiple sites in our network!

Action Report

The Action Report is a tool I built to give my Learning Team visibility on all of the individual training records needed per associate, and to also ensure training was completed and met all compliance

Ambassador Tracker

As a learning Trainer, It was my job to properly keep tabs on all of the Ambassadors that assisted me in my training. At any given time, we have over One Hundred associates working for us as training ambassadors. This tool was designed to help my learning team keep track of our ambassadors, their availability, and skill sets

Attendence Helper

This super simple tool packs quite a punch! As a trainer, we often needed to conduct mass skill sets trainings in a given window, off of a given list. A common barrier was determining whom was on site to receive said training. I built and designed this tool so we could identify whom was on site, and with the use of a departments board scan, determine their location as well
